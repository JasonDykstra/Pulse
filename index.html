Earth is dying. In the planet's final days, the world's leading scientists launched a probe containing all the necessary equipment to rebuild human society elsewhere. The probe's destination: anywhere else.

Many years passed uneventfully... The vast emptiness of space seemed hopeless. After 120 years of scouting, the probe sensed strange signals - radio waves that peaked at regular intervals. Hope seemed near.

As the probe approached, the strange signals seemed to be coming from an exoplanet, previously unknown to humanity. The probe descended to enter into orbit, and was almost knocked off-course by the planet's chaotic atmosphere. In an effort to terraform the planet into something more habitable, the probe descended on several sites.
